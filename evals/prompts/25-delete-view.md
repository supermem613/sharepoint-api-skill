## Task

Delete the view created/updated in evals 23–24. After deleting, retrieve the views collection and confirm the view is gone.

## Checks

- [ ] Used sp-post.js with X-HTTP-Method DELETE to remove the view
- [ ] Called the correct view endpoint by title or GUID
- [ ] Verified the view no longer appears in the views collection
- [ ] No errors during deletion
